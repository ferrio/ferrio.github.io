Grand Palladium Riviera Maya

The Grand Palladium Riviera Maya complex consists of four 5-star hotels on the Riviera Maya with direct access to Kantenah beach. All hotels have access to the facilities, comprising 9 themed restaurants, 5 buffet restaurants, 25 bars, 9 swimming-pools, a magnificent Spa, sports facilities, events and conference rooms, disco, Baby Club (1 to 3 years), Palladium Mini Club (4 to 12 years), Black & White Junior Club (13 to 19 years).
We have reserved blocks of rooms from 03/02/2022-03/16/2022 in 2 sections of the Grand Palladium Riviera Maya, The Colonial and the TRS. The Colonial is the family section and the TRS is the adults only section. Guests choosing to stay in either of the sections can access all amenities, restaurants, beaches, pools, etc. of the other sections in the resort (except for children in the TRS sections).
The resort is all-inclusive, which means that all your food, drinks (including top shelf liquor when requested), 24 hour room service, mini bar with liquor, mixers, juice, water etc. in all rooms and is refilled every day, non-motorized activities at the beach and pools, kids/teens clubs and activities, and much more, are all included in the daily rate. It's pretty amazing to know what you'll be paying for your entire trip and not have to worry about how much you drank/ate and get that surprise bill when you check out!
